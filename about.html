About Foodies Toastmasters

Where culinary passion meets public speaking excellence

Our Secret Recipe

Founded in 2023, Foodies Toastmasters is a unique District 106 club blending the art of public speaking with the love of all things culinary. We're the first Toastmasters club specifically for food professionals and enthusiasts!

Our Mission

To provide a supportive learning environment where food lovers develop communication and leadership skills through deliciously themed meetings.

Our Vision

A world where every culinary professional can present their ideas as beautifully as they plate their dishes.

Our Values

Flavorful feedback, well-seasoned speeches, and a welcoming table for all food enthusiasts.

Our Meeting Flavors

We meet twice monthly with a delicious variety of formats:

1

Monthly Club Meeting

Traditional Toastmasters meeting with food-themed roles and evaluations, held at Larpenteur Estates party room and via Zoom.

2

Monthly Social Gathering

Casual networking at local eateries where we practice impromptu speaking over great food (think "Table Topics with Tapas").

Hybrid Format

All meetings are accessible both in-person and virtually. Zoom link provided upon RSVP.

Our Special Ingredients

Food-themed meeting roles that make learning extra flavorful:

Chef de Cuisine

(Toastmaster of the Day)

Prepares the meeting's agenda and ensures all elements come together harmoniously.

Maître D'

(General Evaluator)

Oversees the evaluation portion of the meeting with hospitality and grace.

Sous Chef

(Evaluator)

Provides thoughtful feedback to help speakers refine their "recipes."

Expediter

(Timer)

Keeps the meeting moving at the perfect pace - not too fast, not too slow.
